Class TCODNoise

noise Base toolkits Noise generator This toolkit provides several functions to generate Perlin noise and other derived noises. It can handle noise functions from 1 to 4 dimensions. Usage example: 1D noise : the variation of a torch intensity 2D fbm : heightfield generation or clouds 3D fbm : animated smoke If you don't know what is Perlin noise and derived functions, or what is the influence of the different fractal parameters, check the Perlin noise sample included with the library.
For example, in 4D, Perlin noise is 17 times slower than simplex noise.
